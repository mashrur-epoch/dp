Dorchester Prestige operates its businesses and luxury services in the heart of Knightsbridge, one of the most desirable and executive districts in central London. We are a boutique agency specializing in marketing luxury properties for sale, rental and property management.

The large collection of houses, apartments and mansions as well as luxury cars, coupled with the assistance of our dedicated team, will offer you the freedom to customize your own London’s life style. All our residences were well tailored to meet with your expectations, from gorgeously designed kitchens and receptions to the latest communication and security technologies.
